Welcome to Groupon's official job site for the UK and Ireland

Groupon is always on the lookout for talented individuals with an entrepreneurial spirit and unmatched drive. Got what it takes? Join the world's fastest growing e-commerce company today, if you:

  • Thrive within a non-bureaucratic environment
  • Proactively pursue both creative and entrepreneurial goals
  • Enjoy the challenging atmosphere of a business start-up and excel under pressure

Have a look at our current vacancies. You can search for a job filtered through various categories and navigate through the current open positions with the tool on the right hand side.

Please do not hesitate to contact us in case you have any questions. Send your queries to jobs@groupon.co.uk or contact us under +44 (0)203 510 2115. Alternatively, please read the FAQs for more information.

To all applicants: There is no application deadline for the featured vacancies. We are hiring now so apply now.
To all recruitment agencies: Groupon does not accept agency CVs. Please do not forward CVs to our jobs alias, Groupon employees or any other company location.
