About

Ultimate Mobile Travel Solutions

MTT delivers sophisticated mobile solutions for leading airlines, hotels, travel management companies and online travel agencies that drive revenue, ensure customer engagement, deepen loyalty and reduce costs. We use our professional mobile travel platform and innovative software products to build mobile experiences that delight travellers and leave them wanting more. MTT first launched mobile bookings for airlines in 2006 and we have remained a first mover in this space ever since.

Experience and Expertise

MTT is the only company dedicated to mobile technology for the travel industry. We are passionate about great mobile travel experiences and have built a company and a culture that delivers on that passion. We value creativity, innovation and diversity of perspectives and experience. Our deep travel industry expertise, rock-solid mobile technology and user centred design process all come together to create engaging, high-performing mobile services for airlines, hotels and travel intermediaries.

Proven Results

MTT powered apps and mobile sites are used by millions of travellers every week worldwide and provide our customers with a strong proven ROI and competitive advantage in the marketplace. MTT apps and mobile sites have won numerous industry awards for their customer focused design and commercial success.

Design

User Focused

The end user is at the heart of everything we do. Our team of designers, graphic artists, QA Specialists and usability experts follow a customer-centric process to define and build the best user experience possible.

Interaction Design

After gathering and analysing requirements, our UX team sketches, wireframes and prototypes the app or mobile website. Every piece of functionality is carefully designed in wireframe format and prototyped on a mobile device to ensure it is as user-friendly, intuitive and as engaging to use as possible.

Visual Design

When we are happy with the low fidelity prototype, our design team adds visual treatment to make our apps really stand out. We are experts at converting brand styles to mobile, ensuring the look, feel and tone of the app closely matches our customers brand messaging, while adhering to best practices of mobile design.

Usability

Because usability is the key to success for all apps, we run regular user testing throughout all stages of design and development. We don’t want our apps to just be usable, we want users to love using them. Regular testing along with an iterative design process ensures our products are perfect from day one.

Design Workshops

We facilitate workshops with our customers to design new functionality. Our design team meets with project stakeholders, the marketing team, and customer advocates on-site to brainstorm and discuss new features and ensure our customers are involved in the process every step of the way.

Intelligent Push Engine

Deliver relevant, timely information to your customers on their device via sophisticated messaging with multi-language support. The MTT Intelligent Push Engine enables comprehensive, personalised push messaging based on trigger events, PNR, CRM and previous purchase & travel history. Messages can be delivered through In App notification, Push notifications, SMS or email.

Mobile Channel Manager

Mobile Channel Manager puts the power to manage and optimise the entire mobile channel directly into the hands of marketing and e-commerce team. Mobile Channel Manager centralises control of each module and the content for each platform so you can manage the addition of content such as promotions or fare sales on your mobile channel without relying on external developers.

Central Information Service – CIS

Enhance your app with smart information from external data sources including images, addresses, maps, social, and other meta content to enhance the user experience. Our CIS will ensure that you can continually enhance your app with a reduced development cost and within minimum timeframes enabling you to rapidly respond to customer needs and improve their overall app experience.

Identity & Security Service – IDS

Our Identity & Security Service allows the creation, storage and managements of user profiles in your mobile services. In-channel profiles support stream-lined user experiences. Robust security and authentication features ensure that your customer data is secure, encrypted and anonymised. IDS is a scalable solution and easily supports millions of subscribers.

            Tech

            OS: We’re Unix nerds. Most of us develop on OS X and some on Ubuntu.

            Client & Server-Side: Lots of Open Source. Mainly Java, Spring, Tomcat, MySQL, some Mongo and Node. HTML5, advanced JS/CSS. On the native client side iOS and Android, some BlackBerry and Windows Phone.

            IDE: We love JetBrains’ Intellij and AppCode. And, the new Xcode is not bad at all.

            QA & Automation: We’re TDD maniacs. Everybody is a tester. Everything we do needs to be tested and automated. Luckily we’ve got great frameworks to help: JBehave, Calabash, Capybara, Mocha.

            Culture

            At MTT we work hard to ensure on-going open communication across the organisation and to ensure all employees understand what is going on in our business, our direction and plans.

            In addition to regular ‘All Hands’ meeting hosted by the CEO, you will have frequent team meetings and participate in many knowledge-sharing sessions with colleagues from all departments.

            We offer very competitive salaries, paid for private healthcare, performance related bonuses and flexible working hours. Our offices in Dublin city are a 15 minute walk from the city centre and minutes from both the LUAS and Heuston station.
